Privacy Policy

How we handle devotee and booking details

PunyaSutra is operated by Qorven Technologies. This policy explains how booking, payment, and contact details are used for puja coordination.

Information We Collect

We may collect your name, phone number, email address, gotra, puja preference, preferred date, booking notes, payment reference, and account login details when you use the website.

How We Use Information

We use this information to create bookings, coordinate puja services, confirm payments, provide tracking updates, respond to support requests, and maintain service records.

Payments

Payments are processed through Razorpay. We do not store complete card, UPI, or netbanking credentials on our website.

Data Sharing

Booking details may be shared with internal team members, priests, or service partners only where needed to perform or support the selected puja.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@punyasutra.com.
