Refunds

Refund and cancellation policy

This policy explains how cancellations and refund requests are handled for PunyaSutra puja bookings.

Cancellation Before Coordination

If you request cancellation before the puja is scheduled or assigned, the team will review the request and process an eligible refund after deducting any payment gateway or administrative charges, where applicable.

After Puja Coordination Starts

Once priest, temple, samagri, or ritual coordination has started, cancellation may not be fully refundable. The eligible amount depends on the work already completed.

Completed Puja

Bookings marked completed are generally not eligible for refund unless there is a verified service issue.

Refund Timeline

Approved refunds are initiated through the original payment method where possible. Bank or payment provider timelines may vary.

Contact

For cancellation or refund requests, contact support@punyasutra.com with your booking reference ID.
